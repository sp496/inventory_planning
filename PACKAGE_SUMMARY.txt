Scenario 1: Patient on Dual Therapy
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Patient receives Sacituzumab Govitecan (4 units) + Pembrolizumab (1 unit)
Visit schedule: Days 1 and 8 of every 21-day cycle

Input:  2 rows in treatment plan (one per drug)
Output: Separate visit records for each drug

Result: Patient 20735-001
        Jan 15: Sacituzumab (4) + Pembro (1)
        Jan 22: Sacituzumab (4) + Pembro (1)
        Feb 05: Sacituzumab (4) + Pembro (1)
        ... continues for 12 months


Scenario 2: Crossover Patient
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Patient started on Nab-Paclitaxel, switched to Sacituzumab Govitecan

Input:  Status = "Crossover Approved"
        TPC = "Nab-Paclitaxel" (original, for reference)
        
Output: All future visits show Sacituzumab Govitecan (current drug)
        Visits labeled as "Crossover Cycle X Day Y"

Result: Patient 10663-106
        Oct 22: Crossover Cycle 2 Day 8 - Sacituzumab (4)
        Nov 11: Crossover Cycle 3 Day 1 - Sacituzumab (4)
        ... continues for 12 months

⚠️  IMPORTANT NOTES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✓ For combination therapy (Drug A + Drug B):
  → Create SEPARATE ROWS in treatment plan
  → One row per drug with same matching criteria

✓ For crossover patients:
  → TPC column shows original drug (for reference)
  → Study Drug Dispensed shows current drug (for forecasting)

✓ Visit Days format:
  → Use: "1,8" or "1,8,15" (comma-separated, no spaces)
  → Don't use: "1, 8" or "1 8"

✓ Date format:
  → Use: YYYY-MM-DD (e.g., 2024-01-15)
